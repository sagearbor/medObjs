Medical School objective DB


A searchable database from multiple national societies


This site was set up to allow searching of medical objectives across discplines. The initial database was populated by objectives suggested for medical school by national societed from multiple disciplines (see table below). Future work may allow for uploading of

  • updated objectives to replace old versions
  • new objectives from socities
  • specific objectives for an institution or individual
  • answers to objecives
